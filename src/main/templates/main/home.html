Convert Sign Language to Text in realtime
Shortening the Gap

There are around 466 million people worldwide have disabling hearing loss, with 34 million of these being children. This number is set to increase to up to 900 million by 2050. We are working on a solution to bridge the accesibility gap between this collective and videos, one of today's main formats of sharing information

User-Centric Design

In order to solve this problem, we aim to place the user at the center of our research, developing solutions with them for them. Our goal is not to apply an interesting technology to X problem but to research how we can better solve a problem (Video translation in ASL) given our existing knowledge in a variety of fields.

Easy to work with

Hard problems don't deserve hard solutions. Our aim is to create a simple solution that can be scaled beyond a scientific paper and that is increasingly adding more and more value to its users as its usage increases. Because of this, we aim to remove as many barriers as possible.
